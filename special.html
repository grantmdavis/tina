Special Events and Parties

For many of life's events, music plays an important role, so much so that we cannot imagine these experiences without it. Tina enjoys sharing her artistry of classical music and musical theater in more intimate settings and performs frequently at special events, ranging from weddings and parties to meetings and fundraisers. Let her voice enhance your event by providing exceptional music at the highest level.

Fine Dining Entertainment

Can one truly get a taste of Italy at an Italian restaurant without hearing a little opera?! Certo, no! On occasion, Tina and her duet partner will bring the vibe up a notch or two with arias and duets from Italy's greatest composers: Rossini, Bellini, Donizetti, Verdi, and Puccini. If you are interested in heightening your guests' dining experience and treating them to a night they will never forget, consider adding a pinch of opera to your menu. Programs have run from 2 selections to 40 minutes and can be structured to accommodate whatever you have in mind.

Recitals for Independent Living Facilities

In addition to performing for special events, Tina performs for independent living facilities so residents can enjoy live music right in their living space. Tina is passionate about making music accessible and integrating it into one's routine. If you are an activities director, please feel free to attend one of Tina's performances. For more information, you can contact Tina or the host facility's activities director. Don't miss out on the chance of wowing your residents with a program that will make a grand impression.

Facilities have included:

  • Covenant Village of Northbrook
  • Oak Trace of Downers Grove
  • Beacon Hill, Lombard
  • The Garlands of Barrington
  • Lake Barrington Woods

Parties

Let your imagination take you to places you have never been through the sound of music. Plan parties around destinations and popular cultures you wish you knew more about. Invite music into your life around the holidays - American or foreign. Or perhaps you would like to hear selections from your favorite composer, be it opera, musical theater or song.

Tina has a passion for native and foreign traditions and welcomes the opportunity to share it with you. Her multi-cultural interests have earned her a minor in German studies, a certificate in Italian language studies and have brought her to Switzerland, Austria, Germany, France, Italy, and Puerto Rico.

Consultation

Once you consult Tina about the details of your special function, she designs a musical program tailored to capture the theme of your event. The resulting ambiance will make your affair unforgettable.

Pricing

A number of factors are taken into consideration when determining service fees. Pricing is determined on a case-by-case basis and is contingent upon the following factors: duration of program, difficulty of repertoire, requested additional singer(s)/instrumentalist(s)/keyboard, rehearsal time with accompanist, pianist fee, and travel distance to the event. Confirm that your venue can provide a tuned piano. If your venue does not provide a keyboard, Tina is happy to provide an electric keyboard for an additional fee.
